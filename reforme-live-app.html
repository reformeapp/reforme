Privacy Policy
Last updated June 2025
1. Information we collect

Reformé collects information you provide directly, including your name, email address, body measurements (weight, height, age), fitness goals, and food log data including meal photos. We also collect usage data to improve the app experience.

2. How we use your information

Your data is used to provide the Reformé service — calculating calorie targets, displaying your food log to your connected trainer, and generating AI-powered insights. We do not sell your personal data to third parties under any circumstances.

3. Food photos

Food photos you take are processed by our AI system to extract nutritional data (calories, protein, carbs, fat). Photos are automatically deleted from our servers within 24 hours of being taken. The nutritional data extracted from your photos is retained as part of your food log history.

4. Trainer access to your data

If you are connected to a trainer on Reformé, your trainer can see your food log entries, calorie and macro data, body stats, wearable data, and streak information. Your trainer cannot see your personal messages to Reformé support or your payment information. You can disconnect from a trainer at any time from your profile settings.

5. Data security

All data is encrypted in transit and at rest using industry-standard encryption. We use Supabase with Row Level Security to ensure users can only access their own data. Your password is never stored in plain text.

6. Data retention

Your account data is retained for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account all personal data is permanently removed within 30 days. Food log history is retained for 2 years to allow you to view long-term trends.

7. Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. You can export your food log data from your profile settings. To request deletion of your account and all associated data, email us at privacy@reforme.fit.

8. Cookies

Reformé uses session cookies to keep you logged in. We do not use advertising cookies or track you across other websites.

9. Children

Reformé is not intended for users under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 16.

10. Contact

For any privacy questions or concerns contact us at privacy@reforme.fit or through the app's Help section.
